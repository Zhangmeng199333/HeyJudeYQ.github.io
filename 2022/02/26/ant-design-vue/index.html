ant-design-vue select下拉框组件

2022-02-26

问题：选中select的某一项，下拉框不显示已选择项？

解决方式：
添加chang事件：

<a-form-item label="状态">
    <a-select v-model="queryInfo.statusCode" placeholder="请选择状态" allowClear @change="selectChange">
        <a-select-option value="voltageStatusScope3">一</a-select-option>
        <a-select-option value="voltageStatusScope2">二</a-select-option>
        <a-select-option value="voltageStatusScope1">三</a-select-option>
    </a-select>
</a-form-item>

selectChange(){
    this.$forceUpdate();
}
